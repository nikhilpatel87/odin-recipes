Cocoa Powder Chocolate Mousse


Description

This mousse served in shot glasses is perfect for when you're craving something sweet but just want a small portion. You can enjoy them as soon as you make them, or you can chill them in the fridge for a cooler dessert.


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp powdered sugar
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
\

Steps:

  1. Combine heavy whipping cream, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt a large bowl and beat with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  2. Pipe mousse into 6 shot glasses. Serve immediately or chill until serving.
